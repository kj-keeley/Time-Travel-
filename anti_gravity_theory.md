## 1. Background: Tesla's Dynamic Theory of Gravity

In 1937, Tesla announced he had developed a "dynamic theory of gravity" that he hoped to give to the world. The theory was never published, and no mathematical details survive. What is known:

- Tesla proposed gravity is a **field effect produced by electromagnetic force**, not spacetime curvature
- The theory was developed between 1893-1894, **simultaneously with his high-frequency current experiments**
- Tesla rejected Einstein's general relativity, arguing that curved space would react on bodies to straighten the curves

This temporal coincidence — high-frequency experiments and gravity theory developed in the same period — suggests Tesla may have observed something in his high-frequency work that he connected to gravitational phenomena.

### Modern Validation of Tesla's Adjacent Work

Tesla's high-frequency therapeutic research has been independently rediscovered as **transcranial alternating current stimulation (tACS)**, confirming the Gold Mountain Pattern: valid research buried under noise, rediscovered decades later under a different name.

Key finding from modern research: tACS at specific frequencies (theta: 4-7 Hz, gamma: 40 Hz) measurably alters neural oscillation patterns and cognitive function. The principle — **external frequency injection modifies internal oscillation patterns** — is exactly what Tesla demonstrated in the 1890s.

## 2. First Approach: Force-on-Force (Failed)

### Hypothesis
If electromagnetic fields can couple to gravitational fields, sufficiently strong EM fields could counteract gravity.

### Evidence
- Theoretical coupling between EM and gravitational fields exists but the coefficient is extremely small
- A 2024 experiment (Nature Scientific Reports) found no anomalous forces in steady-field EM-gravity coupling tests
- The study noted that **high-frequency domain coupling remains unexplored**

### Why It Failed
The coupling coefficient is too small. Even with high-frequency amplification, the energy required to produce measurable gravitational effects is astronomical. **Fighting force with force doesn't work when one force is 10^36 times weaker than the other.**

## 5. Third Approach: Delaying Crystallization (Most Promising)

### The Breakthrough Insight

If wood grain is being created at the cambium (present moment), and if the grain pattern is not yet determined at the moment of creation, then **preventing crystallization eliminates grain formation entirely**.

No grain = no gravity in that region.

### Analogy

| Action | Energy Required |
|---|---|
| Breaking hardened concrete (fighting existing gravity) | Enormous |
| Reshaping wet cement (rewriting emerging gravity) | Moderate |
| Preventing cement from being poured (delaying crystallization) | **Minimal** |

### Mechanism: Decoherence Prevention

In quantum mechanics, states become definite (crystallize) through **decoherence** — interaction with the environment. If decoherence is prevented, the quantum state remains in superposition. Applied to spacetime:

```
Decoherence → bit confirmation → information pattern → gravity emerges
Prevent decoherence → no bit confirmation → no pattern → no gravity
```

This is already achieved at microscopic scale: **quantum computers maintain coherence** by isolating qubits at near-absolute-zero temperatures, preventing environmental interaction.

The challenge is scaling this from qubits to macroscopic spacetime regions.

### Connection to Tesla

Tesla's high-frequency oscillations rapidly alternate the state of matter. If a region of spacetime is subjected to sufficiently rapid oscillation, **the state may never settle into a definite configuration** — like a die that never stops spinning. The "grain" never crystallizes because the material is in constant flux.

```
High-frequency oscillation → continuous state change
→ decoherence prevention at material level
→ local gravity reduction
```

This is speculative but connects established physics (high-frequency state manipulation) to the theoretical framework (entropic/informational gravity) through the Tree Structure analogy.

## 6. Natural Evidence: Cosmic Voids as Tree Hollows

### Tree Hollows: How They Form

Tree hollows are almost never self-generated. They are caused by:
1. **Fungi** — decompose heartwood from inside (most common)
2. **Insects** — consume wood, creating tunnels
3. **Physical damage** — broken branches allow fungi entry
4. **Aging** — heartwood loses moisture and crumbles

Common pattern: **hollows form where something consumed or prevented grain formation**.

### Cosmic Voids: The Same Pattern?

If cosmic voids are analogous to tree hollows, they are not "empty by design" but rather **regions where something consumed or prevented spacetime structure formation**.

This reframes the question:
- Standard cosmology: "Why is there no matter in voids?"
- Tree Structure: **"What consumed or prevented the grain in these regions?"**

### Observable Predictions

If this framework is correct:
- Void boundaries should show evidence of "grain consumption" — anomalous energy signatures or information-theoretic properties
- The Eridanus Supervoid + CMB Cold Spot alignment may represent a region where crystallization was historically disrupted
- Voids should not be perfectly uniform inside — if caused by "consumption," there should be remnant patterns (like tunnels left by insects)

### Implications for Anti-Gravity

If nature has already created "grain-free zones" (voids), **the mechanism for anti-gravity already exists in the universe**. The task is not to invent it but to understand and reproduce it. Studying void boundaries may reveal the natural process that prevents spacetime crystallization.
